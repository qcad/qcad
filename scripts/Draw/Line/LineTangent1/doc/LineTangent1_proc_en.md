1. Choose the desired
[line type](../../doc/Line_en.html#type) in the options
toolbar.
2. Use the mouse to specify the location of the start point of the line or
enter a coordinate in the command line.
3. Click the entity to which you want to create the tangent. Usually, two
tangents are possible. When moving the mouse around, you can see a preview of
the tangent that will be created.
