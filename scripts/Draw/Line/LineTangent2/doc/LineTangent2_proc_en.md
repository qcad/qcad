1. Choose the desired
[line type](../../doc/Line_en.html#type) in the options
toolbar.
2. Click the first arc or circle entity to which you want to create the
tangent.
3. Click the second arc or circle entity to which you want to create the
tangent. Usually, four tangents are possible. When moving the mouse around, you
can see a preview of the tangent that will be created.
